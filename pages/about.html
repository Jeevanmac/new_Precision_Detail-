Our Journey

2012

The Vision

Marcus Thorne begins his journey with a mobile detailing kit and a singular dream: to bring showroom-quality perfection to every driveway. Our foundational philosophy of precision was born in these early days of obsessive service.

2014

First Boutique Studio

Opening our first physical boutique studio in the heart of the city. We began specializing in high-end luxury and vintage cars, implementing dust-free environments and specialized lighting arrays to detect the finest surface imperfections.

2016

Nanotech Integration

Embracing nanotechnology and becoming a certified installer for the world's leading ceramic coating brands. We redefined surface protection by introducing multi-stage paint correction coupled with advanced glass coatings.

2018

Global Recognition

Featured in international automotive journals for our proprietary "Diamond-Cut" paint correction technique. Precision Detail became a destination for automotive collectors across the region seeking unrivaled reflection quality.

2020

Masterclass Academy

Launching our internal training academy. Marcus began mentoring the next generation of master detailers, ensuring that our "Precision Standard" remains consistent even as our team of experts continues to grow.

2022

Advanced Facilities

Opening two new state-of-the-art facilities equipped with hospital-grade air filtration and climate control systems. These environments allow for the most stable and perfect curing conditions for our high-end coatings.

2024

Digital Evolution

Launching our bespoke digital experience platform. This allows our VIP collectors to track their fleet's maintenance history and detailing milestones through a secure, luxury-focused digital dashboard.

2025

The Future

Continuously pushing the boundaries of chemical engineering and surface protection. Our journey continues as we redefine what's possible in automotive care for the next generation of enthusiasts.
