Event Coverage

Precision Invitational 2024

David Miller

Master Detailer & Tech Lead

calendar_today March 15, 2024
schedule 10 min read

Our biggest event of the year, the Precision Invitational, brought together over 100 supercars and the finest detailing enthusiasts from around the country.

The atmosphere was electric as the gates opened at 9:00 AM. A symphony of high-performance engines filled the air, with everything from classic Italian thoroughbreds to the latest German hypercars. This year, we wanted to showcase more than just clean cars—we wanted to showcase the *art* of preservation.

Live Demonstrations

Our team spent the afternoon performing live multi-stage paint correction and ceramic coating demonstrations. We believe that seeing the process first-hand is the best way for our clients to understand the level of detail we put into every square inch of paintwork. The crowd watched in awe as we transformed a swirl-marked panel into a deep, mirror-like finish using our proprietary Precision Compound.

1

Technical Workshop

Exclusive session for our Platinum members covering the latest in paint protection film (PPF) technology and ceramic maintenance.

2

The Detailing Lounge

A relaxation hub for car owners to exchange stories over premium espresso while viewing our gallery of restoration projects.

As the sun began to set, we handed out the "Concours Excellence" awards. It wasn't just about the rarest car, but the best-maintained one. We want to thank everyone who joined us for making the Precision Invitational 2024 an unforgettable experience. See you on the track next year!
